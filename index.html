Why study with the University of Professional Studies, Accra?

The University of Professional Studies, Accra (UPSA) was established in 1965 and has since then grown and advanced to become a progressive public institution that provides academic and professional higher education. UPSA has achieved the reputation as the oldest Ghanaian human resource development institution in professional Accountancy and Management with many of its graduates in key leadership positions in Ghana and abroad. We strive to provide and promote quality higher education and training in management and other related disciplines by leveraging a structured mix of scholarship and professionalism.

UPSA is fully accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) in the USA.
